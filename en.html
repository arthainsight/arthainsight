The loop you cannot get out of

You have built something. You carry responsibility — for a business, a team, or a career. You are used to solving problems on your own.

But this one does not resolve. Every time you think it through, you end up back in the same place. The same arguments. The same doubts. The same sense that something essential is still being missed.

You cannot get out because you do not see what is keeping you there. Your own assumptions are the hardest to notice — they rarely feel like assumptions while you are inside them.

More analysis does not help. More information does not help. You need someone outside the situation who can hear what is there, ask the questions you are not asking yourself, and help make visible what has stayed hidden in plain sight.

How I work

90 minutes. One issue. No slides, no frameworks, no tools.

You lay out the situation. I listen — not just to the words, but to what sits behind them.

I ask questions. I do not comfort and I do not advise. I work as a mirror and a probe — reflecting your thinking back to you as it is, and bringing to the surface the assumptions you have not yet seen as assumptions.

Most of the time, what matters is already there. It is just covered by explanations, assumptions, or fear.

By the end of the session, you will see more clearly where you actually are — and what that means next.

Frequently asked questions

What does sparring at Artha Insight mean?

Sparring is a 90-minute 1:1 conversation focused on a situation where you feel stuck. It is not coaching, mentoring, or therapy. I do not teach, advise, or give you tools. My role is to help you see your own thinking and assumptions more clearly.

Who is this for?

This work is for entrepreneurs, leaders, and experts who are used to solving problems on their own, but have a situation that keeps looping. A decision you cannot make. A direction that does not become clear. Or the sense that you are doing the right things for the wrong reasons.

How is this different from coaching or business advisory?

Coaching and business advisory often build a process, offer frameworks, and support you over time. I work with one issue at a time. I do not hand you a ready-made answer, but I make visible what is actually in front of you so you can see your situation clearly and decide from there.
