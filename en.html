Who I am

Riku Forsell

Thinking Clarity Partner

Gambling addiction forced me to ask questions I had never dared to ask. Who am I really? Why is this happening? Who is the self I imagine myself to be?

It triggered a spiritual awakening. And clarified something essential: my life's purpose is to help people — through speaking, writing, sparring.

That's where the journey began. First as a massage therapist, because it was the fastest path to entrepreneurship. I knew even then that it was a stopover. For the past year and a half, I've been building this — and living through that same stuckness that you might be suffering from right now.

I've been an entrepreneur since 2019. I know what it's like to make decisions alone, without certainty of where they'll lead. I know what it's like to search for direction even when it doesn't seem to exist.

When I listen to someone, I often hear what they don't say out loud. I see the loop they're stuck in — before they've finished saying it themselves.

That's where I can help.
