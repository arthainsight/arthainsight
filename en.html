If you recognize yourself in this

You have walked a long road. You have skill, responsibility, and life experience. But right now something is hidden in fog — a direction, a decision, a meaning, or something you cannot yet put into words.

How I work

We meet remotely, one at a time. Sessions last 90 minutes.

You tell me what is on your mind. I listen carefully. I ask the questions that the answers require — often the ones you have not yet dared to ask yourself. I put into words what I hear.

I do not give ready-made answers. They already exist within you. My work is to help you find them.

Who I am

Riku Forsell

38 years old, based in Porvoo, Finland

I did not come to this work through formal training. My turning point was a gambling addiction — that was the beginning of my spiritual awakening and my desire to understand myself more deeply. The addictions did not end in that single moment, and they have returned in many forms since. But that first turn was the doorway through which I entered a different kind of inquiry.

Since then, I have spent years studying spiritual traditions, philosophies, and the workings of the human mind. I trained as a massage therapist, but I knew within myself that it was not my dharma — my path is to share what I have learned, to speak, and to help people understand their own lives.

Still, I stayed stuck for a long time. I went to many consultants and coaches looking for answers about what I should do. I did not get them — neither from them nor from myself. I was not listening to the inner voice that had been there all along. Life felt meaningless precisely because of that.

Now I have begun to hear that voice. And it tells me that it is time to do what I came here to do: help, speak, share. My work is sitting across from another person, hearing them, and helping them find their own voice — the same one I silenced in myself for too long.

I believe the answers are within you. My work is to help you hear them.
