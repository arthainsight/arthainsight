The loop you can't break out of

You've built something. You carry responsibility — for a company, a team, a career. You're used to solving problems on your own.

But this one you can't solve. Every time you think it through, you come back to the same place. The same arguments. The same doubts. The same feeling that something essential is still out of sight.

You can't get out because you can't see what's keeping you in. Your own assumptions are the hardest to see — you don't notice them as assumptions.

More analysis won't help. More reading won't help. You need someone outside who listens and asks the questions you don't ask yourself.

How I work

90 minutes. One topic. No slides, no frameworks.

You tell me your situation. I listen — not just to the words, but to what's behind them.

I ask questions. I don't comfort or advise. I act as a mirror and an excavator — I reflect your thoughts back to you as they are, and I surface the assumptions you haven't recognized as assumptions.

Most often, what matters is already known. It's just buried under explanations, assumptions, or fear.

By the end of the session you'll have a clearer view of where you actually are, what you assume about your situation, and which thoughts are keeping you stuck. What you do with that view is your responsibility.

Two weeks later we meet for a 20-minute follow-up to see what has moved.

Frequently asked questions

What does sparring at Artha Insight mean?

Sparring is a 90-minute 1:1 conversation where we work through a situation you're stuck in. It's not coaching, mentoring, or therapy. I don't teach, advise, or provide tools. My role is to help you see your own thoughts and assumptions more clearly.

Who is this sparring for?

Sparring is for entrepreneurs, leaders, and experts who are used to solving problems on their own — but some situation has started running in circles. A decision you can't make. A direction that won't clarify. A feeling that you're doing the right things for the wrong reasons.

How is this different from coaching or business training?

Coaching and business training build process, provide tools, and walk alongside you for longer. I work with one topic at a time. I don't guide you in any direction — I don't know what's right for you. My work is to make your thinking visible to yourself.
